Privacy Policy

Mealwise · Last updated 19 June 2026

Home Privacy Support

Mealwise stores everything on your device. We don't require an account and we never sell your data.

Overview

Mealwise is a meal planning and shopping list app made by Scotford Studios. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Data stored on your device only

All meal plans, recipes, ingredients, and shopping lists you create in Mealwise are stored exclusively on your device. This data is never transmitted to us or any third party. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

If you choose to add photos to your meals, these are also stored locally on your device only.

Crash and error reports

Mealwise uses Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports and error logs. This helps us identify and fix bugs. Sentry may collect your device type, OS version, and a stack trace of the error. No personally identifiable information is included. You can review Sentry's privacy policy at sentry.io/privacy.

What we don't collect

  • We do not require you to create an account
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not use advertising or tracking
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

Children's privacy

Mealwise does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted at this URL.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at ascotford@gmail.com.
