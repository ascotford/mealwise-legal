Common questions

Frequently asked

Tap the + button on the Meals tab to add a new meal. You can give it a name, add ingredients, set a default serving size, and attach a photo.
Plan your meals for the week on the Planner tab and Mealwise automatically builds your shopping list from the ingredients. Items are grouped by aisle to make your shop faster.
Shopping Mode gives you a clean, full-screen view of your list while you're in the supermarket. Tap an item to check it off as you go.
All your data is stored on your device. If you delete the app your data will be lost, so make sure your device is backed up to iCloud before uninstalling.
When planning a meal, you can adjust the number of servings and Mealwise will scale the ingredient quantities on your shopping list automatically.
